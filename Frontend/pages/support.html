Excel Logistics FAQs

  1. 1. What services does Excel Logistics offer?

    Excel Logistics provides a wide range of services including freight forwarding, warehousing, inventory management, last-mile delivery, and supply chain consulting. We specialize in both domestic and international logistics solutions.

  2. 2. How can I track my shipment?

    You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided at the time of dispatch. Visit our Tracking Portal and enter your tracking ID to get real-time updates on your package status.

  3. 3. Does Excel Logistics support Excel-based reporting tools?

    Yes! We offer Excel-compatible templates for inventory tracking, delivery schedules, and performance metrics. These tools help logistics managers streamline operations and analyze data efficiently.

  4. 4. Which Excel functions are most useful for logistics management?

    Some of the most valuable Excel functions for logistics include:

    • VLOOKUP and INDEX-MATCH for data retrieval
    • SUMIFS and COUNTIFS for conditional calculations
    • IF, IFERROR, and TEXTJOIN for error handling and formatting
    • MAX, MIN, and AVERAGE for performance analysis

  5. 5. Can I integrate Excel with my logistics software?

    Yes, many logistics platforms support CSV or XLSX exports/imports. Excel can be used to clean, analyze, and visualize logistics data before uploading it to your system.

    • Missing values in lookup tables
